More than one thing to do

If you are building out FUNdusky the right way, family fun cannot be one generic category. This page should help people quickly see the real spots worth checking out.

Indoor Favorite

Ghostly Manor

One of Sandusky’s easiest all-in-one family stops. Great when you want indoor fun, a group activity, or something that works even when the weather does not. A strong fit for families, teens, and anybody looking for more than just one attraction.

Fast & Competitive

Go-Karts & Mini Golf

Fast, competitive, and easy for families to say yes to. This is the kind of stop that works for kids, parents, friend groups, and visitors who want something active without needing a full-day commitment.

Food + Fun Combo

Bowling & Laser Tag at Thirsty Pony

Thirsty Pony is not just food and drinks. It also gives families and groups another reason to stop in with bowling, laser tag, and built-in entertainment that works before or after eating. This is a strong “everybody can do something” option.

Older Kids & Groups

Axe Throwing

If you want something a little more competitive and a little less typical, axe throwing is a solid add to the family and group category. It fits especially well for older kids, parents, mixed-age groups, and people looking for a different kind of night out.
